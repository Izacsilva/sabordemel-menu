Tokens — Espaçamento & Shape

Grid de 8px. Border-radius seguindo hierarquia de componente.

Grid de Espaçamento (8px base)

4px — micro gap (half step)
8px — small
12px — card gap
16px — medium
24px — section gap
32px — page padding

Border Radius

50% / rounded-full — chips, badges, qty buttons
rounded-3xl (24px) — modal, cart drawer
rounded-2xl (16px) — product cards, buttons
rounded-xl (12px) — thumbnail, inputs, size chips
rounded-lg (8px) — cart item icon, minor
